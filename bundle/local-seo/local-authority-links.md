Links still matter for local organic rankings, but the bigger shift is towards **brand mentions and earned media** — especially for AI visibility. Ahrefs' 75,000-brand study found brand web mentions correlate far more strongly with AI Overview visibility (≈0.664) than backlinks do (≈0.218) — roughly three times the strength. It's a correlation study, not proof of causation, but the direction is clear: get talked about, not just linked to.

# What to check

* Local authority signals on the page — Chamber of Commerce, BBB, local press, sponsorships, community involvement.
* Brand mentions across the wider web (not just links).
* Placement on credible local "best of" / round-up lists.
* A steady trickle of quality local links rather than spikes.

# Scoring guide

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Full | Visible local authority signals (chamber, BBB, press) and evident community presence. |
| Partial | Some signals but limited local link/mention footprint. |
| Low | No detectable local authority signals. |
