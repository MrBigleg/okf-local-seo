BrightLocal's annual Local Consumer Review Survey (LCRS). Verified figures used in this bundle:

* **68%** of consumers won't use a business rated under 4 stars (up from 55% the prior year).
* **31%** require a minimum of 4.5 stars (up from 17% the prior year).
