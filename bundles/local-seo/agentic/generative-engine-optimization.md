Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of preparing content and profile data so generative AI systems can confidently include a business in synthesised answers. Applied to local, it targets [Google Business Profile](/local-seo/gbp-signals.md) and Maps, emphasising specific, chunked descriptions and authentic media a model can reason over.

# Local GEO signals

* Specific GBP service and product descriptions.
* Clear business-description copy naming category, service area, proof, and differentiators.
* [Local landing page](/local-seo/local-onpage.md) copy with concrete service, location, review, media, and schema facts.
* Review themes that mention use cases, ambience, offerings, service quality, and constraints.
* Authentic user photos with descriptive captions/context.
* Consistent item/service names across profile, website, and schema.
* Clear booking, reservation, or ordering paths.

# GEO vs AEO

Answer Engine Optimization focuses on retrievable facts and direct answers. GEO focuses on *inclusion* in generated recommendations, summaries, and conversational responses. Both need clean source data; GEO adds weight on media, reviews, and qualitative attributes.
