Ahrefs analysed ~75,000 brands to see what correlates with AI Overview visibility. Verified headline finding: **brand web mentions correlate far more strongly (≈0.664) than backlinks (≈0.218)** — roughly 3× the strength. Note this is a *correlation* study across AI Overviews generally, not a controlled or local-specific test. Cited in [local links & authority](/local-seo/local-authority-links.md) and [AI search impact on local](/local-seo/ai-search-local.md).
