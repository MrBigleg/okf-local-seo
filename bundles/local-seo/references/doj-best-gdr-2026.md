DOJ press release (11 May 2026, release 26-474) and the filed complaint in the US District Court for the Northern District of Illinois against B.E.S.T. GDR, LLC, d/b/a Premium Home Service, and its founder and CEO Yosef Bernath. Filed on referral from the FTC, jointly with the Illinois Attorney General. Primary source for [spam & fake listings](/gbp/spam-and-fake-listings.md).

# What the source establishes

* The press release states the defendants created **over 15,000 fake business profiles on Google Search and Maps**, using addresses of unrelated businesses and local numbers routed to overseas call centres, plus fabricated five-star reviews using images misappropriated from unrelated websites' staff directories.
* The filed complaint alleges the scheme ran **since at least 2018**, deceived **well over 100,000 consumers**, took **"tens of millions of dollars"**, and operated **over 7,600 telephone numbers across more than 250 area codes**.
* Alleged violations: the FTC Act, the FTC Rule on the Use of Consumer Reviews and Testimonials, the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act, and Illinois consumer-protection statutes. Relief sought: permanent injunction, civil penalties and consumer redress.

# Clarification carried into the bundle

A widely circulated **"~$79 million"** gains figure is not in the press release, the FTC release, or the filed complaint; it originates in investigative journalism (KARE 11). The bundle uses the complaint's own wording ("tens of millions of dollars"). The CEO's name is spelled **Yosef** (one "s") in the filings.
