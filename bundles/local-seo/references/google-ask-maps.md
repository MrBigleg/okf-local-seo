Google's primary-source announcement for **Ask Maps**, its Gemini-powered conversational experience in Google Maps. This is the single source underpinning the [Ask Maps](/agentic/ask-maps.md) concept doc.

# What the source establishes

* Ask Maps is a Gemini-powered conversational experience reached from an **Ask Maps** button. The announcement does not specify a durable on-screen position for that button.
* Announced **12 March 2026**, rolling out on Android and iOS in the **United States and India**, with desktop described as coming soon.
* Inputs Google names: information on more than 300 million places; reviews and local knowledge from more than 500 million contributors; the user's searched and saved places; and route context including directions and ETAs.
* The experience can help a user act on a result — reserve, save, share or start navigation.

# What it does not establish

Google has not published Ask Maps ranking factors. Profile-completeness and media practices are data-quality measures, not guaranteed optimisation levers. See [Generative Engine Optimization](/agentic/generative-engine-optimization.md).
