Google's primary-source documentation for **Business Agent**, cited by [Agentic Commerce Readiness](/agentic/agentic-commerce-readiness.md).

# What the source establishes

* Business Agent is a **Search** experience for eligible **US-based e-commerce retailers**.
* Customisation requires: a US-based store, a verified **Merchant Center** account, **at least 50 approved free listings**, and a **claimed brand profile**.
* It draws on Merchant Center data, the retailer's website and additional supplied business data.

# Clarification carried into the bundle

Google's Search **brand profile** and **Google Business Profile** are separate surfaces. A brand profile is not another name for a Business Profile. The "~50 offers" figure used in earlier drafts is corrected to **at least 50 approved free listings** plus the other conditions above.
