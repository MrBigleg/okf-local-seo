Google's announcement post "Connect with local holiday shoppers" (Matt Madrigal, VP/GM Merchant Shopping, 4 November 2021) — the primary source for the GMB→GBP rename. The post is filed under ads-commerce rather than the small-business section, which is why it is often missed. Google's Business Profile community announcement of the same change ("Introducing new updates to Business Profile") carries the same wording with extra operational detail. Cited from [Google Business Profile](/gbp/google-business-profile.md).

# What the source establishes

* "To keep things simple, 'Google My Business' is being renamed 'Google Business Profile.'"
* Businesses can claim, verify and manage the profile directly on Google Search or the Google Maps app.
* The Google My Business app was slated for retirement in 2022.
* The existing GMB web experience transitioned to supporting larger multi-location businesses as "Business Profile Manager"; the community announcement adds that the Google My Business API would be renamed the Business Profile API with functionality retained.
