Google's help-centre documentation of Business Profile verification. Primary source for [GBP verification](/gbp/verification.md).

# What the source establishes

* Six verification methods as of the access date: video recording, phone or text, email, live video call, postcard, and instant verification via Google Search Console.
* "Verification methods are automatically determined by Google and can't be changed"; the options offered depend on business type, public information, region and business hours.
* Post-submission review takes **up to 5 business days**; verification codes expire after 30 days; most postcards arrive within 14 days.

# Clarification carried into the bundle

Internal notes previously said *five* methods; the current help page lists six (Search Console instant verification is the addition). The bundle states six.
