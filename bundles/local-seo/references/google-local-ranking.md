Google's help-centre statement of how local results are ranked. Used as the primary source for the relevance / distance / prominence framework in [Google Business Profile](/gbp/google-business-profile.md).

# What the source establishes

* Local results are based primarily on **relevance** ("how well a Business Profile matches what someone is searching for"), **distance** ("how far each business is from the customer who's searching") and **prominence** ("how well-known a business is").
* Prominence explicitly considers how many websites link to the business and how many reviews it has.
* "There's no way to request or pay for a better local ranking on Google."
