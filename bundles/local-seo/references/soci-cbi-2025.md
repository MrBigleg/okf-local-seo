SOCi's 2025 Consumer Behavior Index, announced 4 June 2025. Survey fieldwork 19–24 February 2025; 1,001 adult US respondents distributed evenly across gender, age and geography. Cited in [reviews on GBP](/gbp/reviews.md).

# What the source establishes

* **91% of consumers rely on peer-generated content to evaluate local businesses** (all respondents). SOCi's index page words the same finding as "91% use reviews to validate decisions".
* **65% say they are more likely to choose a business that actively responds to reviews** — presented in SOCi's release in the context of younger audiences, so the bundle scopes it to younger consumers rather than all respondents.
* Related findings: 40% of younger consumers prefer video reviews; 55% report growing scepticism of fake feedback.

# Clarification carried into the bundle

Internal notes attributed both headline figures to all consumers. The 91% figure is all-respondents; the 65% figure is scoped to younger audiences in the primary release, and is cited that way here.
